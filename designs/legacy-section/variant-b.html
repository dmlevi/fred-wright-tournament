In Honor Of
“A REMEMBRANCE OF FRED” Frederick J. Wright Jr. (1898–1958) was an amateur of the first rank — a member of the winning 1923 United States Walker Cup team, seven-time Massachusetts state champion, and the 1956 USGA Senior Amateur champion. He was still competing for the National Amateur on the morning of August 26th, 1958, when he collapsed at the thirteenth tee of The Country Club at Brookline. This tournament is played each spring in his memory.
— The Board of Governors
